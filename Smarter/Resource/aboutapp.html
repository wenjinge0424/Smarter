About the App

Smarter is an app that integrates education and technology. In a world where social media, online gaming and cellphones have become an overwhelming distraction. Smarter allows you to put education first. Smarter allows parents to intermittently interrupt their children's device usage with academic age appropriate questionnaire. Teachers can connect with students and parents, by allowing their students to follow them on smarter and adding study guide questions, that are relevant to the curriculum they are using that day. Teachers have the option to sell these guides through smarter to either other teachers or parents and students. Parents, students and teachers will have access to the results, allowing everyone to have a clear understanding of their progress. Smarter is about putting education first.
